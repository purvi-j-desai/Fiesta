About Fiesta



Fiesta is distributed social platform that helps websites embed rich social experiences without writing a single line of code. To accompany the platform, Fiesta has built-in modules such as Get-a-fan, Viral Offer Sharing, Ask-a-friend, Share-a-purchase. Websites can enable these social modules on their pagesÂ  without writing a single line of code or modifying their own website. Benefits of Fiesta are improved conversion, increased sales and viral social reach.


Passive social experiences such as placing Facebook and Twitter buttons on your website usually provide very poor engagement. Modifying your visitors normal workflow to get social reach distracts the visitor and leads to a drop in conversion. With Fiesta, you can inject rich social experiences without modifying your visitors workflow.


In 2012, Fiesta will continue to develop new social apps/modules as a part of their platform. As new apps/modules are made available, websites can add these apps/modules to their workflows without any page modification. It takes a simple Javascript tag addition to enable the Fiesta platform on a website. Fiesta's platform is a multi-layered architecture that can scale massively to meet the needs of millions of websites.
